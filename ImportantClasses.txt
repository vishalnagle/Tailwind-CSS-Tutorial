Here’s a list of **important and commonly used Tailwind CSS utility classes** categorized for easy reference:  

---

### 🔹 **Layout & Box Model**  
📏 **Spacing (Margin & Padding)**  
- `m-4`, `mt-2`, `mb-3`, `ml-5`, `mr-6`, `mx-auto`, `my-2`  
- `p-4`, `pt-2`, `pb-3`, `pl-5`, `pr-6`, `px-8`, `py-10`  

📐 **Width & Height**  
- `w-full`, `w-screen`, `w-1/2`, `w-1/3`, `w-1/4`, `w-auto`, `w-96`  
- `h-full`, `h-screen`, `h-1/2`, `h-96`, `h-auto`  

📌 **Max & Min Width/Height**  
- `max-w-sm`, `max-w-md`, `max-w-lg`, `max-w-xl`, `max-w-full`  
- `min-w-0`, `min-w-full`, `min-w-max`, `min-h-screen`  

📦 **Display & Visibility**  
- `block`, `inline`, `inline-block`, `hidden`  
- `flex`, `grid`, `table`, `contents`, `flow-root`  
- `invisible`, `visible`  

📜 **Overflow**  
- `overflow-auto`, `overflow-hidden`, `overflow-scroll`  
- `overflow-x-auto`, `overflow-y-hidden`  

🔳 **Positioning**  
- `static`, `relative`, `absolute`, `fixed`, `sticky`  
- `top-0`, `bottom-0`, `left-0`, `right-0`  
- `z-0`, `z-10`, `z-50`, `z-auto`  

📍 **Flexbox & Grid**  
- `flex`, `inline-flex`, `grid`  
- `flex-row`, `flex-col`, `flex-wrap`, `flex-nowrap`  
- `justify-start`, `justify-center`, `justify-between`, `justify-end`  
- `items-start`, `items-center`, `items-end`  
- `gap-2`, `gap-4`, `gap-6`, `gap-x-4`, `gap-y-2`  
- `grid-cols-1`, `grid-cols-2`, `grid-cols-3`, `grid-cols-12`

🌈 **Text Colors**  
- `text-black`, `text-white`, `text-gray-500`, `text-red-500`, `text-blue-600`  

🖌 **Background Colors**  
- `bg-white`, `bg-black`, `bg-gray-200`, `bg-blue-500`, `bg-green-400`  

🔆 **Opacity**  
- `opacity-0`, `opacity-50`, `opacity-100`  

🌟 **Shadows & Borders**  
- `shadow-sm`, `shadow-md`, `shadow-lg`, `shadow-xl`, `shadow-2xl`  
- `border`, `border-2`, `border-4`, `border-solid`, `border-dashed`  
- `border-gray-500`, `border-blue-600`, `border-red-300`  

✏ **Rounded Corners & Outline**  
- `rounded`, `rounded-md`, `rounded-lg`, `rounded-full`  
- `outline`, `outline-none`, `outline-blue-500`

- `bg-cover`, `bg-contain`, `bg-auto`
